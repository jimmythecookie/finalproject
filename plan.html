The Plan for AI in Education

  1. Set Clear Rules: Create strict guidelines to make sure AI is used fairly and ethically in schools.
  2. Keep Data Safe: Ensure that students' personal information is protected from unauthorized access.
  3. Teach Digital Skills: Make sure students and teachers know how to use AI tools effectively.
  4. Ensure Everyone Can Access AI: Make AI tools available to all students, no matter their background.
  5. Check If AI Tools Are Working Well: Regularly check the AI tools to ensure they are helping and not causing problems.
